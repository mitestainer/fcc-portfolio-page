About me

Thanks for stopping by. This is the place where you can find my projects and get to know me better. I'm 26, Brazilian (can't play soccer well though), digital marketing enthusiast and tech geek. Wrote my first line of code when I was nine (it was my name inside a h1 tag lol) and spent some years playing around with the pages I used to save from the internet - I kind of liked making them look the way I wanted.

Eventually, I stopped coding for some reason until I got back at the beginning of 2018. I rediscovered how fascinating coding can be. Now I can not just write on plain HTML, but I can style my pages using CSS and I'm also giving my first steps into JS. Hopefully, I'll get even better at this as the time goes by!
